ACTIVITY MONITORING DISCLOSURE

TaskFlow Desktop monitors your work activity while the timer is running.

WHAT WE TRACK:
- Keyboard and mouse activity levels (counts only, not keystrokes)
- Which applications are in the foreground (e.g., "VS Code", "Chrome")
- Active vs idle time during work sessions

WHAT WE DO NOT TRACK:
- What you type (no keystroke logging)
- Screenshots or screen recordings
- Websites visited, files opened, or URLs
- Mouse positions or click coordinates
- Any data when the timer is stopped

HOW IT WORKS:
- Activity data is collected ONLY while your timer is running
- Data is sent to your organization's TaskFlow server every 5 minutes
- Your admin can view activity reports on the TaskFlow web dashboard
- When the timer is stopped, zero monitoring occurs

By installing and using this software, you acknowledge that your organization
uses activity monitoring during tracked work sessions.

Contact your IT administrator for the full privacy policy.
